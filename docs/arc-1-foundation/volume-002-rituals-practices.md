## PART I — HEALING RITUALS

### RITUAL 1 — The Sanctuary Ritual

**Purpose:** Grounding, overwhelm, emotional turbulence

**Light Configuration:**
- Color: Ivory
- - Motion: Soft halo, slow fade
  - - Purpose: Bring user back to safety and presence
   
    - **Symbolic Gesture:** Cathedral "closes doors" to outside noise
   
    - **Invocation:** "I enter the sanctuary. Here, I am safe."
   
    - **Process:**
    - 1. Recognition of overwhelm
      2. 2. Conscious retreat to safety
         3. 3. Grounding through presence
            4. 4. Return when ready
